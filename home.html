DxOrders is designed to simplify your healthcare experience.

Dx Orders is an app to help patients simplify their healthcare experience. Reminders of doctor’s orders, refilling prescriptions and managing basic health conditions right on your phone.

How It Works

  • Manage Your Prescriptions

    >

    Managing a bunch of medicine is a pain. When do I take them? Did I forget to take one this morning? Do I need to get a refill? Manage all of that right on your phone to make sure your doing what you need to.

  • Manage Your Care Providers

    Need to get in touch with your doctor, or even find a doctor? Do you also to need to manage a seperate visit to your OB/Gyn, or maybe a physical therapist or even a cardiologist. Keep them all connected on your terms so you can remember all your appointments with no hassle

  • Manage Your Goals

    >

    Everyone has goals about being healthy, but how do you get there and what does that even mean? We use your clinical medical data to help set realistic goals that will help you improve your overall health.
